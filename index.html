Top Nature Phenomenons

Aurora borealis

The northern lights, or aurora borealis, are a spectacular, colourful display of light commonly seen in the night sky in the northern hemisphere. Auroras in the southern hemisphere are known as the southern lights, or aurora australis.

Lenticular clouds

Lenticular clouds are stationary, lens-shaped clouds that often appear near mountains, formed when stable air flows over a barrier like a mountain range, causing the air to rise and condense into a cloud with a distinctive "flying saucer" appearance

Read more View images

Flammable ice bubbles

During spring, the ice thaws, and the bubbles pop, releasing the encased methane into the atmosphere. Since this gas is highly flammable, the bubbles can explode when set alight.
